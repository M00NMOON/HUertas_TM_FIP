About Couples Resort Rooms

Experience luxury at our resort with spacious 1400 sq ft accommodations featuring private outdoor hot tubs. Indulge with a complimentary Espresso/Americano machine in every room. Relax in your private sauna and double shower with four shower heads and benches. Plus, enjoy the added convenience of a bidet and microwave.
