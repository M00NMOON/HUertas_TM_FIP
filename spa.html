Spa Policies and Information

Spa Policies and Information

We offer 6 Spa Treatment Rooms. Please wear loose, comfortable clothing for facials. For Body Molding and Body Wraps, wear your robe and undergarment and change in the Treatment Room.

Appointments are available from 9:00 am to 9:00 pm for up to 2 people, subject to accommodation size and Spa Practitioner availability.

Cancellations and no-shows within 12 hours will be charged 100% of the treatment price. Pre-book appointments for a successful Algonquin Spa Experience; while we strive to accommodate, unforeseen circumstances may arise.

Treatments are charged to your account with 13% HST included. We have a NO EXTRA TIPPING policy.

Rare cancellations due to staff emergencies may occur, but we work to find alternatives or offer a free 1-hour Spa Treatment Voucher for your next stay.
